# ParasiteArena

A Unity-based wave survival game where players fight off lots of enemies, level up, and gain new abilities.

## Features & Systems

- **Wave System**: Progress through difficult waves, encountering bosses at specific intervals.
- **Possession System**: Different enemy mechanics including possession abilities.
- **Stat System**: Level up, open chests, and purchase upgrades from the shop to improve player stats.
- **Combat & AI**: Enemy behavior and fighting mechanics.

## Architecture

The managers and controllers include:
- `PlayerController` - Handles player movement and actions.
- `WaveManager` - Controls enemy spawning and wave progression.
- `LevelUpRewardManager` - Manages rewards and stat improvements when leveling up.
- `EnemyAI` & `PossessionSystem` - Manage enemy
